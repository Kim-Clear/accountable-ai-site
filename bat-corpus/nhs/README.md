# NHS Workflow Corpus

This folder contains NHS and NHS England published workflow examples where AI
appears to influence clinical prioritisation, routing, recommendation,
communication, documentation, or action.

The initial entries were created from a user-provided Perplexity extraction and
checked against the linked public NHS/NHS England source pages where available.
They should be treated as early corpus entries for BAT testing, not final
methodology findings.
