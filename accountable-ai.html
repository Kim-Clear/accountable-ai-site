About Kim Stevens

I'm experienced working in regulated environments where technology, data, and accountability intersect.

My background in biomolecular science and project delivery has shaped the analytical approach I bring to new technologies like AI..

I focus on understanding how tools interact with data, workflows, and real-world business processes.

I created the CLEAR Framework to help small businesses introduce AI in a way that is structured, practical, and proportionate.

Experience in regulated environments

Before focusing on responsible AI adoption, I spent several years managing complex projects in clinical research and other regulated environments.

I also have experience supporting the evaluation and rollout of AI tools in a professional workplace, including helping teams understand how tools such as Microsoft Copilot can be used responsibly and effectively.

That experience reinforced something important: successful AI adoption is rarely just about the technology itself. It is about understanding workflows, data, accountability, and the expectations around how tools should be used.

The same structured approach now shapes how I help small businesses introduce AI with practical guardrails, clear workflows, and proportionate governance.
